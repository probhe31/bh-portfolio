About me

I am a Systems Engineer with a Master's degree in Software Engineering. I specialize in the development of interactive software, video games, and web solutions, combining creative thinking, interaction design, and technical execution.

I work with technologies like Unity, C#, React, and FastAPI to build products that are functional, visually polished, and technically solid. I am interested in generating ideas, developing playable concepts, and designing experiences that connect well with their users.

I also have experience in university teaching, instructing on game development, programming, and interactive experience design.
